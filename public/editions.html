Not merchandise. Artefacts from the House of Jettset — created in limited capsules, placed quietly within our circle, and designed to be lived in rather than simply owned.

The Capsule

Made To Be Earned, Not Simply Bought

Editions begins in strict black and white. Heavyweight materials, restrained branding and details that reveal themselves slowly — closer to premium apparel and considered travel goods than corporate gifting. The purpose isn't to place a logo on more objects. It's a small number of pieces that feel unmistakably part of the House. Once a capsule is complete, it's complete.

The Editions Standard

Nothing Added Without Purpose

Limited By Design

Quantities are defined before release and kept intentionally small.

Built To Be Used

Every piece must earn its place through function, wearability or relevance to the journey.

Quietly Recognisable

Branding remains restrained. Recognition belongs to those who know the House.

Culture Before Commerce

Editions exists to deepen the Jettset world, not to become a conventional merchandise line.

Scarcity is not a marketing tactic here. It's the point. Editions is seeded to those already moving with us before it is ever offered for sale.
