01 — Private Aviation

Jet Card

Guaranteed access, structured around how you actually fly. A prepaid arrangement that trades ad-hoc uncertainty for a year of fixed-rate clarity.

Your Jet Card secures aircraft access and pricing stability for twelve months — built for guests who fly often enough that certainty matters more than spontaneity.

Coverage

Three Primary Regions

Europe

Primary Service Area

United Kingdom, France, Germany, Switzerland, Italy, Spain, Portugal, Greece, the Nordics, and Central & Eastern Europe — included within standard Jet Card hourly pricing.

United States

Primary Service Area

All fifty states, including Alaska and Hawaii, with coverage extending to Canada, Mexico, Puerto Rico, the U.S. Virgin Islands, and core Caribbean destinations.

Middle East

Primary Service Area

UAE, Saudi Arabia, Qatar, Kuwait, Oman, Bahrain, Jordan, Egypt, Israel, the Maldives, and the Seychelles. Secondary regions offer further flexibility, subject to operational conditions.

Pricing Structure
Prepaid Hours × Hourly Rate

Flight time is measured from take-off to landing and billed in precise increments — no surge pricing, no hidden costs, no surprises at settlement.

Designed For

Is Jet Card Right For You

  • You fly privately on a recurring basis — typically 25 to 75 hours a year.
  • You value pricing certainty over the flexibility of one-off charter.
  • You want guaranteed aircraft access without the capital commitment of ownership.
  • You move between Europe, the United States, and the Middle East regularly.

If your flying pattern is occasional or highly irregular, Charter may be the more natural fit. We're glad to talk through which structure suits your journey.

Settlement

Prepaid hours can be settled however suits you — by traditional transfer, or in cryptocurrency, powered by MoonPay.
