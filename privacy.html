Privacy Policy

01 What We Collect

To deliver a journey built around you, our guest profile typically includes:

  • Identity details — full name, date of birth, nationality, passport number and expiry, place of birth, gender
  • Contact details — email, phone number, billing address
  • Journey details — routes, dates, passenger and pet information, baggage and catering requirements
  • Preferences — favourite destinations, aircraft likes and dislikes, hotel preferences, dietary needs, and special requests, recorded to personalise future journeys
  • Sensitive travel details, where relevant — pre-existing medical conditions affecting fitness to travel, and security or press-sensitivity considerations you ask us to record
  • Communications — enquiries, booking correspondence, and support requests

02 How We Use It

Your information is used solely by Jettset to respond to enquiries, arrange and manage journeys, provide concierge and membership experiences, and maintain your guest profile so that each journey reflects what we already know about you. Where you have agreed, we may also share relevant updates. We do not sell your information to third parties.

03 Sharing With Operators & Partners

To arrange your journey, we share only the details necessary — such as passenger names, travel dates, and any relevant medical or fitness-to-travel information — with the aircraft operator, ground transport providers, and other suppliers directly involved in delivering your experience. We do not share your information with operators or partners beyond what is required to deliver the journey you have requested. Our team follows a strict internal principle never to release sensitive guest information unnecessarily.

04 Payments

Where payment is made by cryptocurrency, processing is handled by our payments partner, MoonPay, under their own privacy and security terms. Jettset does not store cryptocurrency wallet credentials.

05 Data Retention

We retain guest information for as long as needed to provide our services, maintain your guest profile for future journeys, and meet legal, accounting, or regulatory requirements. You may request deletion of your data at any time, subject to any retention obligations we are required to meet by law.

06 Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data, and to object to certain uses of it. If you provide information on behalf of other passengers or family members, you confirm you have their consent to share it with us. To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details below.

For privacy questions or data requests, contact info@jettsetglobal.com.
