Jettset Europe Ltd · Operating Posture & Safety Standards

01 Our Role

Jettset is a private aviation broker. We do not own or operate aircraft ourselves, and we do not hold an Air Operator's Certificate. Every journey is flown by a licensed, third-party aircraft operator holding the appropriate certification for their jurisdiction — typically FAA Part 135 in the United States, or the equivalent Air Operator's Certificate under EASA's Air Operations framework in Europe. Jettset's role is to source, coordinate, and manage your journey on your behalf — never to blur that line with the role of the operating carrier.

02 Operator Vetting

We work toward a preferred panel of operators, each evaluated against legal authority, insurance coverage, safety certification (including ARGUS, Wyvern, or IS-BAO status where relevant), incident history, and service consistency. Operators are expected to hold current Air Operator's Certificates and to meet applicable international and regional safety standards. Continuous review forms part of how we maintain that panel, rather than a one-time check.

03 Empty Legs & Legs Members

Legs gives members privileged sight of live empty-leg opportunities — it is access to inventory, not a guaranteed booking. Empty legs arise when an aircraft repositions, can appear at short notice, and may be withdrawn or changed if the underlying operator movement changes. We aim to support members with alternative options when this happens.

04 Pricing Transparency

Where we state that pricing is passed through at operator rate, this means Jettset does not apply an additional markup on top of the operator's quoted rate for that specific journey, distinct from any separate membership, concierge, or service fees that may apply.

05 Guest Information & Discretion

Privacy and press-sensitivity considerations are recorded as part of your guest profile where relevant, alongside any security or medical considerations that affect how your journey is delivered. We follow a strict internal principle never to release sensitive guest information unnecessarily, and never to overpromise on what a journey can deliver.

06 If Something Goes Wrong

In the event of a delay, cancellation, or operational disruption, our team works to find alternative solutions as quickly as possible, and to keep you informed throughout. Specific recovery and refund terms depend on the nature of the journey and the operator involved, and will be confirmed in your booking terms.

For questions about our operating standards, contact info@jettsetglobal.com.
