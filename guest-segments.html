Guest Segments

Six worlds, one standard of movement. Every guest arrives with a different definition of what trust looks like — we've built around all six.

Private aviation has always served old wealth. Jettset was built for the new guard too — without asking either to compromise.

01

Traditional HNW & UHNW Principals

Cares about: Privacy, continuity, safety, family readiness, peak access
Breaks trust: Inconsistent aircraft, opaque sourcing, security concerns

A private aviation chief-of-staff — direct operator sourcing, a named lead, full preference memory, and security-grade discretion.

02

Family Office & Executive Assistants

Cares about: Speed, SLAs, policy compliance, invoice clarity, repeatability
Breaks trust: Slow quoting, messy paperwork, no escalation path

A single desk for board travel, family office movement, and special missions — SLA-backed, with clean reporting throughout.

03

Founders & Entrepreneurs

Cares about: Flexible short-notice, multi-city movement, productive cabins
Breaks trust: Bureaucratic booking, poor connectivity, limited availability

Founder-speed global access — frictionless booking, onboard productivity, and routing that follows your calendar, not the other way round.

04

Recording Artists & Touring Teams

Cares about: Reliability, entourage handling, late-night flexibility, security
Breaks trust: Missed slots, weak recovery, poor instrument or baggage handling

Tour-grade mission control for talent, management, and VIP guest movements — built for the road, not against it.

Sourced through relationships with artist managers and touring agencies, not cold outreach.

05

Athletes & Sports Executives

Cares about: Event logistics, privacy, recovery windows, family handling
Breaks trust: Game-day chaos, media exposure, poor ground coordination

Performance-first travel, built around fixtures, recovery, and secure arrival-to-departure handling.

Built alongside sports agencies and tournament hospitality groups who know the calendar before it's public.

06

Influencers & Cultural Tastemakers

Cares about: Visual polish, status signalling, fan-safe confidentiality
Breaks trust: Generic luxury tone, privacy leaks, inflexible content rights

A culturally fluent aviation brand — one that understands launch windows, entourage needs, content boundaries, and partner activations.
