03 — Club

Club

Not a loyalty scheme. A standing relationship — for guests and offices who return to Jettset often enough that the relationship itself deserves structure.

Club is built around two relationships, not six tiers — one for the individual guest, one for the office that moves on their behalf.

Two Tiers

Built Around How You Return

For The Individual Guest

Deposit Wallet Membership

  • Guaranteed response times on every enquiry
  • Sourcing fees waived across all journeys
  • Priority recovery if a journey is disrupted
  • Access to lifestyle perks and curated events
  • A standing deposit, drawn down as you fly
For Family Offices & EAs

Corporate Retainer

  • A named account lead for every enquiry
  • SLA-backed quoting and response times
  • Clean, consolidated invoicing for the office
  • Policy-compliant booking for boards and principals
  • Built for repeatable, multi-traveller movement

Club exists to deepen a relationship already built on trust — it is not where the relationship begins. Most guests join Club after their first journey with us, not before.
