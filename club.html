03 — Membership

Club

Not a loyalty scheme. A standing relationship for guests and offices who return to Jettset often enough that the relationship itself deserves structure.

Club brings continuity to the way you move. It gives returning guests, and the people who travel on their behalf, a familiar team, agreed response standards and an account structure designed around repeated journeys. It does not replace Charter or Jet Card — it strengthens the relationship around them, carrying knowledge, preferences and accountability from one journey into the next.

The Club Standard

What Every Member Relationship Includes

A Familiar Team

Continuity

A consistent point of contact across aviation and supporting arrangements. Journey history and stated preferences carried forward. Less repetition for returning guests, assistants and authorised bookers.

A Defined Response

Priority

Priority handling of aviation and concierge enquiries. Response expectations agreed as part of the membership structure. Earlier escalation when timings change or a journey is disrupted.

A Cleaner Account

Clarity

A clear structure for deposits, retainers, invoicing and authorised bookers. Transparent journey confirmations and cost visibility, across one relationship.

Service That Learns

Recognition

Preferences remembered rather than repeatedly requested. The team understands the principal, the office, and the context around the journey.

Two Memberships

Built Around How You Return

Club is intentionally simple. One relationship for the individual guest, one for the office responsible for moving principals, teams or multiple travellers.

For The Individual Guest

Deposit Wallet Membership

  • Guaranteed response standards on every enquiry
  • Sourcing fees waived across eligible journeys
  • Priority handling when travel is time-sensitive
  • Priority recovery if a confirmed journey is disrupted
  • A dedicated guest profile carrying preferences forward
  • A standing deposit, drawn down as travel is booked
  • Selected lifestyle privileges and curated Jettset events

Designed for guests who charter repeatedly but don't need a fixed-hour Jet Card — founders, artists, athletes, families and executives whose schedules change quickly.

For Family Offices & EAs

Corporate Retainer

  • A named account lead with ownership of the relationship
  • SLA-backed response and quoting standards
  • Authorised-booker controls for assistants and offices
  • Policy-aligned sourcing for boards and principals
  • Consolidated invoicing and clearer reporting
  • Centralised traveller profiles and operational notes
  • Priority escalation for short-notice or disrupted movement

Designed for family offices, executive assistants, artist management and companies moving boards, executives or delegations who need discretion and clean administration.

Choosing The Right Structure

Club, Charter Or Jet Card?

Each solves a different need. They can also work together.

CharterJet CardClub
Best forIndividual journeys and changing requirements.Regular flyers who want prepaid hours and pricing structure.Returning guests or offices who want continuity and account structure.

Club is not built around points, status symbols or artificial tiers. Its value is practical: a team that already knows the relationship, an account that's ready to move, and a standard that doesn't need renegotiating every time. Club exists to deepen a relationship already built on trust — it is not where the relationship begins. Most guests join after their first journey with us, not before.
