Legs

Empty leg journeys, surfaced to those who can move on short notice. A members-only window into live aircraft repositioning — at operator price.

When an aircraft needs to reposition without passengers, the empty sector may become available at a significantly reduced rate. Legs gives Jettset members privileged access to those journeys as soon as they enter the market. The route, departure window and aircraft are determined by the underlying flight, so flexibility matters — for members able to move quickly, the opportunity can be exceptional. For guaranteed access on a route and schedule of your choosing, Charter or Jet Card remains the more dependable structure.

The Member Advantage

The Empty-Leg Benefit Stays With The Member

Legs is not a public deal board. Access is reserved for Jettset members, and the benefit created by the repositioning journey is passed through to them.

Members-Only Inventory

Empty-leg opportunities are surfaced within the Jettset membership environment, rather than promoted as open-market charter.

The Full Empty-Leg Saving

The reduced economics of the repositioning sector are reflected in the member price, not converted into standard charter pricing.

Early Visibility

Members can receive opportunities as soon as they are released, giving the best possible window to respond.

Priority Alerts

Route and regional preferences matched to alerts by app, SMS, email or WhatsApp.

Clear Pricing

The whole-aircraft member price is displayed before booking, together with the key conditions attached.

A Known Relationship

The same Jettset team remains available to coordinate the flight and anything required around the journey.

The Portal

Designed For Flexibility

Who Legs Works Best For

  • Members with flexible travel dates.
  • Guests able to use nearby departure or arrival airports.
  • One-way travellers whose route matches an aircraft repositioning.
  • Members comfortable confirming at short notice.
  • Families, principals and teams able to travel together on a whole-aircraft basis.

Legs is most valuable when the traveller can adapt to the aircraft, rather than requiring the aircraft to adapt to the traveller.

Worth Knowing

Privileged access to live inventory — not a guarantee of a specific route, date, or flight.

Empty legs arise because an aircraft is already scheduled to reposition. They are not created around a member's requested route. They can appear at short notice, are commonly one-way, and may change or be withdrawn if the underlying primary journey changes. A Legs opportunity is only confirmed once the licensed operator accepts the booking. When certainty matters more than flexibility, we'll recommend Charter or Jet Card instead.
