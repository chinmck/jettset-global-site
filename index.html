The Fleet Question

What "No Fleet Bias" Actually Means

We don't own aircraft, so we're not trying to fill one. Every journey is matched to a category, not a hangar.

Category

Light Jet

~4–7 Guests · Up To ~3h

Regional hops and short city pairs, where speed to the aircraft matters more than cabin size.

Category

Midsize Jet

~6–9 Guests · Up To ~5h

Full stand-up cabins and more baggage room, for journeys that outgrow a light jet's range.

Category

~8–10 Guests · Up To ~7h

Transatlantic-capable on many routes, with a cabin built for working as easily as resting.

Category

~10–14 Guests · 8h+

Nonstop intercontinental journeys, with a cabin arranged for genuine rest along the way.

Figures are typical, not guaranteed — every seat count, range, and cabin layout is confirmed against the specific aircraft sourced for your journey.

04 — Legs

Legs

Empty leg journeys, surfaced to those who can move on short notice. A members-only window into live aircraft repositioning — at operator price.

An aircraft repositioning empty is an opportunity, not a guarantee. Legs gives our guests privileged sight of it — the moment it appears. For guaranteed access on your own schedule, our Jet Card programme remains the more dependable structure.

The Portal

What Legs Members See

  • Live search by airport, city, or region
  • Flexible date windows, not fixed-day only
  • Nearby-airport radius search
  • Whole-aircraft journeys clearly distinguished
  • Aircraft type, Wi-Fi, pet, and catering details
  • Time-sensitivity and cancellation-risk indicators
  • Instant alerts by app, SMS, email, or WhatsApp
  • Full price transparency, operator rate to guest rate
Worth Knowing

Legs is privileged access to live inventory — not a guarantee of a specific route, date, or flight.

Empty legs arise when an aircraft repositions. They can appear at short notice, are usually one-way, and may be withdrawn if the underlying journey changes. Members move quickly because the opportunity is real — and because it may not last.

The Jettset Story

Where It Began

Jettset was born from a simple observation: for an industry built around luxury, private aviation often felt surprisingly impersonal.

Our founder experienced this first-hand, travelling extensively for business across the world by private jet. The aircraft itself was exceptional. Everything around it wasn't.

A booking would be confirmed. A payment link would arrive. The journey would be arranged. And then the guest was left to organise everything else — transport to the airport, collection on arrival, accommodation, ground logistics, dining, every last-minute request. For the level of investment being made, the experience felt incomplete.

Private aviation had become focused on the aircraft, rather than the journey. The experience shouldn't begin when a guest steps onboard. It should begin the moment they leave their front door.

At the same time, friends, business associates, and people across our founder's network began asking for help arranging flights and managing their travel. What started as helping others quickly revealed a real gap in the market — guests didn't simply want access to aircraft. They wanted someone to think about everything else.

  • The car already waiting outside their home
  • The preferred scent inside the vehicle
  • Their favourite music already playing
  • The flowers, refreshments, and atmosphere that made it feel personal
  • The preferred meal waiting onboard — not a standard menu
  • The chauffeur waiting on arrival
  • The hotel already prepared before they arrived
  • A fresh shirt waiting in the room before an important meeting
  • The restaurant reservation, already secured
  • The details handled before they ever needed to ask

That philosophy became Jettset — built around the belief that true luxury isn't about more choices. It's about fewer decisions.

Today, Jettset combines private aviation with concierge and lifestyle management to create seamless, end-to-end journeys for guests across the world — from charter and helicopter transfers to luxury accommodation, exclusive experiences, security, ground transportation, and bespoke requests of every kind. Every detail is curated around the individual.

Built For What's Next

A new generation of private aviation guests has emerged — entrepreneurs, athletes, artists, creators, founders, global executives. People who move fast, live globally, and expect an experience that reflects who they are. For too long, this generation was underserved by an industry built around traditional models of wealth and legacy guests. Jettset was created for them.

Because the future of private aviation isn't simply about where you're flying. It's about how you feel from the moment you leave home until the moment you arrive.

To give successful people the ultimate luxury: time.

Through private aviation, elite concierge, curated access, and an unreasonable attention to detail, Jettset becomes the trusted partner behind extraordinary lives — not just the company that books the flight.

Jettset Europe Ltd · Operating Posture & Safety Standards

01 Our Role

Jettset is a private aviation broker. We do not own or operate aircraft ourselves, and we do not hold an Air Operator's Certificate. Every journey is flown by a licensed, third-party aircraft operator holding the appropriate certification for their jurisdiction — typically FAA Part 135 in the United States, or the equivalent Air Operator's Certificate under EASA's Air Operations framework in Europe. Jettset's role is to source, coordinate, and manage your journey on your behalf — never to blur that line with the role of the operating carrier.

02 Operator Vetting

We work toward a preferred panel of operators, each evaluated against legal authority, insurance coverage, safety certification (including ARGUS, Wyvern, or IS-BAO status where relevant), incident history, and service consistency. Operators are expected to hold current Air Operator's Certificates and to meet applicable international and regional safety standards. Continuous review forms part of how we maintain that panel, rather than a one-time check.

03 Empty Legs & Legs Members

Legs gives members privileged sight of live empty-leg opportunities — it is access to inventory, not a guaranteed booking. Empty legs arise when an aircraft repositions, can appear at short notice, and may be withdrawn or changed if the underlying operator movement changes. We aim to support members with alternative options when this happens.

04 Pricing Transparency

Where we state that pricing is passed through at operator rate, this means Jettset does not apply an additional markup on top of the operator's quoted rate for that specific journey, distinct from any separate membership, concierge, or service fees that may apply.

05 Guest Information & Discretion

Privacy and press-sensitivity considerations are recorded as part of your guest profile where relevant, alongside any security or medical considerations that affect how your journey is delivered. We follow a strict internal principle never to release sensitive guest information unnecessarily, and never to overpromise on what a journey can deliver.

06 If Something Goes Wrong

In the event of a delay, cancellation, or operational disruption, our team works to find alternative solutions as quickly as possible, and to keep you informed throughout. Specific recovery and refund terms depend on the nature of the journey and the operator involved, and will be confirmed in your booking terms.

For questions about our operating standards, contact info@jettsetglobal.com.
