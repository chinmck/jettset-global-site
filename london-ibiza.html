Signature Route

London to Ibiza

Private charter between London and Ibiza, arranged operator-direct and built around your calendar rather than a published schedule.

Few routes carry the same shift in register as London to Ibiza — a commute for some, a season-opener for others, and for most guests, the moment a week of obligations gives way to a week that doesn't have any. Jettset arranges this route privately and discreetly, departing from the London airport that suits your morning and landing close enough to the island's villas and ports that the journey itself barely registers.

Flight Time
2h 35m

Typical non-stop flight time, London to Ibiza, subject to routing and aircraft type.

Typical Aircraft
Light to Midsize Jet

Citation XLS, Phenom 300, or comparable — sized to the route and your party.

London Departure Points
Luton · Farnborough · Biggin Hill

We source from whichever London-area airport suits your departure best.

What To Expect On This Route

Ibiza's airport runs at its busiest during the summer season, and commercial scheduling reflects that — narrow windows, frequent delays, and little flexibility if your plans change. Private charter removes that constraint entirely. Departure times are built around you, not a timetable, and the aircraft is sourced from operators we work with directly rather than through a layer of resellers.

Most guests on this route are arriving for a specific window — a villa booking, an event, a handful of days that don't stretch to accommodate commercial connections. We coordinate ground transport on both ends as standard, so the journey from your door to the villa happens without a second booking to think about.
