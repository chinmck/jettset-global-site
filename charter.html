The Fleet Question

What "No Fleet Bias" Actually Means

We don't own aircraft, so we're not trying to fill one. Every journey is matched to a category, not a hangar.

Category

VLJ & Light Jets

~4–6 Guests · Up To ~3h

Regional hops and short city pairs, where speed to the aircraft matters more than cabin size. Select models extend to ~3.5h.

Category

Midsize Jets

~8–9 Guests

Full stand-up cabins and more baggage room, built for longer regional travel and extended multi-city journeys.

Category

Heavy Jets

4–6h · Extended Long-Haul

Capable of extended long-haul routes, most efficient with a full cabin — significantly more space, baggage capacity, and onboard amenities.

Category

Ultra Long Range

~16–18 Guests · Intercontinental

Built for nonstop long-haul and intercontinental routes, with the range, comfort, and performance genuine global travel demands.

Category

Group Charters

Full Cabin · Group Sizes

Whole-aircraft charters for larger parties — teams, delegations, and touring groups moving together on one aircraft.

Category

Helicopter

Point-To-Point · Short Hop

Direct transfers where a runway isn't the fastest way in — arranged with the same discretion as every other journey.

Figures are typical, not guaranteed — every seat count, range, and cabin layout is confirmed against the specific aircraft sourced for your journey.
