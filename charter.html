Charter

Private journeys, sourced through Jettset's global fleet network — combining our partner fleets, direct operator relationships and access to the wider international charter market.

Every Charter journey begins with you. Jettset brings together established partner fleets, direct relationships with licensed operators and the wider global charter marketplace — the consistency of trusted fleet partnerships with the reach of an international operator network. Rather than limiting a journey to one source, we identify the aircraft best suited to your itinerary. For guests who fly more regularly, our Jet Card offers the same considered sourcing with the structure of pre-purchased hours.

The Fleet Question

One Network. Every Category.

The Jettset fleet is a global network made available through our partner fleets, direct operator relationships and the wider international charter market — one trusted relationship, with access across multiple categories, regions and operators.

Category

VLJ & Light Jets

~4–6 Guests · Up To ~3h

Well suited to regional journeys and short city pairs where efficient access and speed matter more than cabin scale. Selected models can operate longer sectors depending on load and conditions.

Category

Midsize & Super-Midsize Jets

~7–10 Guests · Regional & Transcontinental

More cabin space, greater baggage capacity and increased range for longer regional travel, multi-city itineraries and selected transatlantic routes.

Category

Heavy Jets

~10–16 Guests · Long-Haul Comfort

Designed for longer sectors with generous cabin space, enhanced baggage capacity and onboard amenities suited to work, rest and extended travel.

Category

Ultra Long Range

~12–18 Guests · Intercontinental

Built for nonstop long-haul and intercontinental journeys, with the range, cabin comfort and performance required for genuine global travel.

Category

Group Charter

Whole Aircraft · Larger Groups

Aircraft arranged for teams, delegations, touring parties, corporate groups and larger families travelling together on one coordinated itinerary.

Category

Helicopter

Point-To-Point · Short Transfer

Direct transfers where road travel or a runway isn't the most efficient route — arranged with the same discretion and journey management as every fixed-wing charter.

Figures are indicative rather than guaranteed. Passenger capacity, range, baggage allowance, cabin configuration and operational suitability are confirmed against the specific aircraft sourced for each journey.
