The Fleet Question

What "No Fleet Bias" Actually Means

We don't own aircraft, so we're not trying to fill one. Every journey is matched to a category, not a hangar.

Category

Light Jet

~4–7 Guests · Up To ~3h

Regional hops and short city pairs, where speed to the aircraft matters more than cabin size.

Category

Midsize Jet

~6–9 Guests · Up To ~5h

Full stand-up cabins and more baggage room, for journeys that outgrow a light jet's range.

Category

Super-Midsize Jet

~8–10 Guests · Up To ~7h

Transatlantic-capable on many routes, with a cabin built for working as easily as resting.

Category

Long Range Jet

~10–14 Guests · 8h+

Nonstop intercontinental journeys, with a cabin arranged for genuine rest along the way.

Figures are typical, not guaranteed — every seat count, range, and cabin layout is confirmed against the specific aircraft sourced for your journey.
